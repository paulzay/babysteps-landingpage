Features

Health records

Vaccination scheduling ensures parents get all important immunizations.

The appointment log helps keep track of check-ups, specialist visits, and health concerns discussed.

Development tracking

Customizable timelines allow parents to input their child's actual achievements, adjusting for premature births or developmental delays.

Growth tracking

Charts visualize the baby's growth over time, allowing parents to see trends.

Personalized alerts

Milestone reminders help parents know what to look out for next

Check-up notifications ensure regular health monitoring.

About

This project was birthed from an idea I had when I looked at my own life and thought what could I build right now that I can use in my own life. I have a daughter and I have been using a baby book to track her milestones and growth. I thought to myself, what if I could build a digital version of this book that could be used by parents all over the world. That's how Babysteps was born. It started as a portfolio project for ALX and I aim to build it into a fully mature app with all the features that parents need to track their child's growth and development

Our team

Our team is made up of a group of passionate individuals (mostly me lol) who are dedicated to helping parents track their child's growth and development. We are committed to providing the best possible service to our customers and we are always looking for ways to improve our product.
